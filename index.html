Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our services

Kya aap advance booking accept karte hain? +

Haan bilkul! Hum 6 months advance tak booking accept karte hain. Wedding season mein jaldi book karna better rahta hai kyunki dates jaldi fill ho jati hain. 25-30% advance payment se booking confirm hoti hai.

Photos aur videos kab milte hain? +

Normal events ke edited photos 10-15 days mein mil jate hain. Wedding photography complete 20-30 days mein deliver hoti hai. Cinematic videos 30-45 days lete hain kyunki editing time lagti hai. Same day highlights bhi available hain.

Drone shoot ke liye extra charges hain? +

Premium aur Luxury packages mein drone shoot included hai. Basic package mein drone shoot ke liye ‚Çπ5,000-8,000 extra charge hota hai. Weather aur location permission ke according final price decide hoti hai.

Bahar ke events cover karte ho? +

Haan, hum Sultanpur district ke alawa nearby cities jaise Lucknow, Rae Bareli, Amethi, Pratapgarh mein bhi shoot karte hain. Outstation events ke liye travel aur accommodation charges alag se lagti hain.

Album design mein kitna time lagta hai? +

Premium quality wedding album design aur printing mein 45-60 days lagta hai. Hum 3-4 design options dikhate hain aur aapki choice ke according final album banate hain. Rush delivery bhi available hai extra charges ke saath.

Live streaming service kaise kaam karti hai? +

Hum YouTube, Facebook ya private platform par live streaming karte hain. HD quality streaming ke saath professional audio setup bhi include hai. Packages ‚Çπ15,000 se start hote hain. Internet connectivity check karke final confirmation dete hain.
